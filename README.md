# Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) (department-of-veterans-affairs)
The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is responsible for providing a wide range of services and benefits to veterans of the United States armed forces and their families. This includes healthcare services, disability compensation, education and training programs, vocational rehabilitation, home loans, burial benefits, and counseling and mental health support. The VA operates a network of hospitals, clinics, and other healthcare facilities across the country to ensure that veterans have access to quality medical care. Additionally, the VA administers a variety of programs aimed at helping veterans transition back into civilian life and achieve financial stability. Overall, the VA plays a crucial role in honoring and supporting the men and women who have served their country in the military.
